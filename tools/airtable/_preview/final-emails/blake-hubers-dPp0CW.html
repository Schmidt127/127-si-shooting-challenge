Be sure the ball does not go too far to the left side of your body. at the Collection area (eye level where both hands raise it to.) If you freeze the video there, you can see that means your right elbow is out a little. This is not a huge deal but the more that you can keep your body in line, the better off and more consistent your shot will be. Toe is the base, knee is over the toe, elbow should be over the knee and wrist should be over the elbow. That is the line you want to maintatin. Not a huge deal, you are shooting with one hand without all that, just something to consider. You are awesome, thanks for shooting. Mike
